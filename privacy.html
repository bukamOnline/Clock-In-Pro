Clock In Pro - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 22, 2026

Nocturnal Devs ("we," "our," or "us") provides Clock In Pro, a productivity app that helps users track work hours, earnings, overtime, and related records. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app and this website.

1. Information We Handle

Clock In Pro is designed to store the work information you enter, such as hours worked, pay rates, earnings, deductions, and preferences, locally on your device. We do not collect or store this user-entered work data on our servers unless you intentionally export, share, or send it yourself.

2. Local Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device using on-device storage technologies. If you uninstall the app or clear its stored data, some or all locally stored information may be deleted.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Your app data is not shared with us unless you choose to contact support, send feedback, or export/share information using features available on your device.

4. User Controls and Deletion

You can review, edit, export, and delete your stored records within the app where those features are available. You can also remove locally stored app data by deleting the app from your device.

5. Third-Party Services

If the app uses platform services or third-party tools, those providers may process limited technical information according to their own terms and privacy policies. We recommend reviewing the privacy information provided by Apple and any third-party services you choose to use in connection with the app.

6. Children's Privacy

Clock In Pro is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the app or this website.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to support secure handling of information, but no device, software environment, transmission method, or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated version on this page and update the Last Updated date above.

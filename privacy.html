Clock In Pro - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 16, 2025

Nocturnal Devs ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed when you use our Clock In Pro mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

The App is designed to function locally on your device. We do not collect or store any personal data on our servers. All information you enter (such as work hours, rates, earnings, and settings) is stored locally on your device only.

2. Local Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using standard browser storage mechanisms (like IndexedDB). This data does not leave your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data stored locally on your device. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. App Permissions

Our App may request certain permissions on your device such as storage access to save and load your data. You can control these permissions through your device settings.

5. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services (such as Google Play Services for distribution or analytics) which may collect information used to identify you. Please refer to the privacy policies of these third-party providers for more information.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

8. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over your data. You can view, modify, export, or delete all data through the App's interface at any time.

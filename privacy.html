Clock In Pro - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Clock In Pro mobile application for iOS and Android and any website or landing page that links to this policy (together, the "Services"). Nocturnal Devs ("we," "our," or "us") explains below how information is handled when you use Clock In Pro.

1. Information You Provide

Clock In Pro lets you enter work-related information such as time entries, breaks, hourly rates, overtime rules, deductions, notes, app preferences, and files you choose to import or export. We do not use our own servers to store your time records; core app records are kept on your device so the app can function.

2. Local Storage and Files

The app stores records locally on your device using IndexedDB for core app data and localStorage for saved app state and preferences. If you export data, files may be saved through your device's file picker, save panel, share sheet, or download tools. If you import data, the selected file is read from storage you explicitly choose.

3. Device, Analytics, Ads, and Update Information

Depending on your device, app version, platform, and installed services, limited technical data may be processed by Android, iOS, Google Play, the Apple App Store, Firebase Analytics, Google Mobile Ads (AdMob), or related platform and service providers. This may include device type, app version, installation source, diagnostics, ad interactions, and basic usage or event data used for app distribution, analytics, advertising, updates, security, and performance.

4. How We Use Information

Information is used to operate the app, save your records on your device, support imports and exports you choose to perform, display ads, provide app updates, respond to support requests, maintain security, and improve the Services.

5. Storage and Retention

Your work records and settings are intended to remain stored locally on your device unless you choose to export, share, or otherwise transmit them. We do not sell your personal information. If you contact us directly, we may keep the information you send us for support and record-keeping purposes.

6. App Permissions and Device Access

The app may request or use access reasonably necessary for its features, such as file selection for imports, file creation, sharing, or downloads for exports, network access for app store services, ads, analytics, and updates, and general device storage used by the app. You can manage applicable permissions through your device settings.

7. Third-Party Services

Clock In Pro may rely on third-party services such as Google Play Services, Google Play In-App Updates on Android, Apple App Store and Apple platform services on iOS, Firebase Analytics, Google Mobile Ads (AdMob), system services, and similar providers. Those providers may process information under their own terms and privacy policies.

8. Children's Privacy

The Services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Your Choices

Because core records are stored on your device, you can review, edit, export, import, or delete your data from within the app. You can also uninstall the app and remove locally stored data through your device settings, subject to any files you exported or backups maintained outside the app.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the revised version on this page and update the "Last Updated" date.

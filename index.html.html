Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private?

Absolutely! All your information stays only on your device using IndexedDB. No account, no cloud needed. You control your data 100%.

Can I change language at any time?

Yes! Instantly switch between English, Spanish, French, Hindi, or German with zero hassle.

Will it work on my Android phone?

100%! TimeTrack Pro is fully optimized for Android devices and can be easily packaged as an app, with native-like experience.

Can I use it without the internet?

Yes, all features work offline. Internet is only ever needed for backups or if you choose cloud export.

Can I track multiple jobs?

Yes! Add work profiles for separate jobs, switch effortlessly, and view separate analytics for each.
